PIKA - The autonomous robot

A perfect pet doesn't exist

New

PIKA is an autonomous robot that can interact with it's surrounding using Image Processing with Machine Learning. It was trained exceptionally to identify me.
It made use of pre-trained MTCNN to detect human faces and dlib for face identification.
It made use of an onboard RaspberryPi4 with a camera sensor to capture images and stream them to a local Desktop via WiFi where the trained ML model was used to classify the images.
It used Arduino MEGA, which was being controlled by the RaspberryPi4 through serial communication, to process data from 5 different censors and to control the motor driver.

NOOB - The noob warrior

NOOB is a RoboWar bot developed by me and my team of 5 others...My job was to develop NOOB's under the hood components...designing all the circuits and the controller.
Since it's development NOOB has participated in various competitons.
The control system comprises of a Radio-wave receiver connected to a customly coded Arduino MEGA.
It works with 3 E-Bike motors(brushed DC motors): 1 500W motor to drive the weapon head and 2 250W motors to drive the wheels.
The body of NOOB is made up of Iron sheets.
NOOB uses one 32 V Lithium-Ion_polymer battery to drive the weapon, four 12V battery to drive the wheels, and one small 12V battery to drive the control system.
It weights 56kgs.


Self Driving car for GTA-V

So I developed a python script to drive cars in Grand Theft Auto 5 PC, not that I was bored of driving car in GTA 5... I still love the game, it was just an idea that hit me to make self-driving cars, ain't got no Tesla in India :/
The script was based on Transfer-Learing
First I drove across all of Los-Santos building a dataset i.e. recording the screen and keystokes.
Then I did edge detection on the images using OpenCV and resized it to 300x300 and converted it to grayscale.
Then I used an InceptionResNetV2 model with input dimention(300x300x1), added a GlobalAvgPooling2D layer and a Dense layer of size 1024 followed by Dropout, followed by a Dense layer of size 3.
Then I trained the model using 'Adam' optimizer.
